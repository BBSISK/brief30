How it works

Simple by design

01
Write
Thirty words. No more. Describe your life as it honestly is right now — not how you'd like it to be.
02
Return
Every ninety days, a snapshot is taken. Come back, read what you wrote, and write again. Only if something has changed.
03
Remember
Over years, a quiet archive builds. The distance between who you were and who you are, measured in words.
👥
Groups
Share your words with a small circle — family, friends, a class. Watch each other change over time.
🌍
Strangers
Opt in to share your current entry anonymously in a public feed. No username. Just thirty words from a real life.
⏳
Time capsules
Seal thirty words for 1, 5, or 10 years. Your future self will open them — and you'll have forgotten what you wrote.
🔑
No password
Choose a memorable username. Get a claim link. That's your key. No email, no account, no friction.
